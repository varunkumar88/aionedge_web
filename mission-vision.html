Key Milestones

Our journey from vision to reality

2025

Foundation and Vision

Founded with a clear vision to simplify complex business processes through the power of AI. Our journey began with the aim to provide innovative, intelligent solutions that streamline operations, boost efficiency, and enable data-driven decisions.

2025

Product Launch

We introduced our first in-house AI products, FLOWAION and CHATAION, to simplify solutions for startups and enterprises alike.

  • FLOWAION: Designed to automate and optimize business workflows using advanced AI analytics, enabling businesses to scale more efficiently.
  • CHATAION: A chat-based AI system that seamlessly integrates with businesses, providing real-time, data-driven insights and automating key tasks.
2025

Simplifying the Dutch Legal Domain

Our products took a major leap forward in the Dutch Legal Domain, where FLOWAION and CHATAION were deployed to streamline and automate complex legal workflows. By simplifying document processing, case law retrieval, and workflow automation, we enabled Dutch legal teams to operate more efficiently and intelligently.

2025

Expanding Across Europe

After successfully launching and gaining traction in the Dutch market, we raised funding to expand our footprint across European markets. This funding has enabled us to enhance our AI tools and scale our impact globally, bringing our vision of AI-powered simplification to more industries and regions.
